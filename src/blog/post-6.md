## My vision

I want to adhere to the indie web movement by making a cool website where I can express my creativity and share cool ideas.
I am building it as a "desktop" inspired interface, where individual pages are represented with intuitive file types like they would in OS:
- .exe for games
- .pdf for my resume
- .md for blog posts
- etc.
